Founders

Maitreyi Rayasam

Maitreyi Rayasam is an enthusiastic learner and teacher, with a growing passion for teaching. She currently teaches mathematics to young students at the Greater Boston STEM program and also tutors children at the educational program Kumon. Additionally, she has completed internships in the fields of Mechanical Engineering, Clinical Trials, Supply Chain, and IT department at the biomedical company Transmedics Inc., which broadened her exposure to working in the corporate field, and expanded her knowledge in computer science. Throughout high school Maitreyi has taken many computer science courses and learned Python, Java, and C++. Her goal is to introduce more students into the STEM field through Code++.
